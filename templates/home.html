About SmartAssist

SmartAssist is an AI-powered virtual assistant designed exclusively for TAMUCC students. It combines intelligent knowledge retrieval, real-time chat assistance, and integrated service tools — all to make your academic life smoother and more connected.

Intelligent Knowledge Base

Search a database of articles, guides, and FAQs instantly.

AI-Powered Chatbot

Service Request & Ticketing

Submit and track your campus service requests easily.

Appointment Scheduling

Book advising or service meetings with ease.

Feedback & Surveys
